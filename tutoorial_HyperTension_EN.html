Senior Home Care Series: Hypertension


This online self-paced tutorial, is designed for all learners who are interested in understanding hypertension, with a special focus on home care.

Intended Audience:
    - Domestic helpers
    - Someone living with people has hypertension.
    - Home care providers.
Learning Objectives:
    Upon completion of this course, you will be able to:
      - understand what is hypertension or high blood pressure.
      - explain the terms systolic, diastolic, and pulse pressures.
      - recognize causes of hypertension.
      - recognize the symptoms of hypertension.
      - list factors that influence blood pressure.
      - demonstrate how to take blood pressure measurements.
