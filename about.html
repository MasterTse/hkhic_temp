About Us

Our Mission

Hong Kong HealthTech Innovation Center (HKHIC) aims at helping people to have a healthier living through innovation and applications of different health related technologies. We help promoting and developing Health Technologies so that the existing healthcare systems could work better for everyone. We are committed to introducing and developing innovative approaches, products, services and solutions that can improve personal health and promote healthier populations across different communities.

 

 

Our Social Responsibilities

We combine our knowledge, business resources, experience and compassion to help improve the health of the communities. We are looking for opportunities for charity, sponsorship and social projects, especially under possible Public Private Partnership (PPP) model.
It is our vision that together we will elevate our partners, customers, suppliers and neighbors to create positive change. To be innovators, leaders and contributors to an inclusive society and a healthy environment so that HKHIC and everyone we touch can endure and thrive.

 

 

Our Efforts

  • Investing on innovative health related technologies and their related teams in different development stages, especially from Hong Kong and the Chinese Mainland, for the promotion of their applications;

     

  • Partnering health technology startups, companies, research institutes and professionals with our support, resources and connections in funding, corporate setup, R&D, product development, commercialization, marketing & sales and distribution of their related technologies in different markets, especially in the Chinese Mainland;

     

  • Providing strategic support and market access for Hong Kong and foreign health technology teams to go into the Chinese Mainland market with our market knowledge and connections there;

     

  • Bridging between the Chinese Mainland and different countries through Hong Kong as an international hub for technology transfer, project financing, market penetration, networking, human resources etc. in health related areas.

     

  • Working with NGOs or social enterprises to make use of available health technologies to serve different communities and social groups with innovative projects;

     

  • Cooperating with governmental bodies and public organizations in the promotion and applications of health technologies, especially in the areas of healthy aging, eHealth applications and development of Hong Kong as super connector for China under the Belt & Road as well as the Big Bay Area Policies.
