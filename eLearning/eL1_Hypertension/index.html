About this Tutorial

This tutorial is designed for all learners who are interested in understanding hypertension with a special focus on home care.



Closed captioning is available

Intended Audience

  • Domestic helpers.
  • Someone living with people has hypertension.
  • Home care providers.

    • Learning Objectives:

  • Understand what is hypertension.
  • Explain the terms systolic and diastolic pressures.
  • Recognize causes of hypertension.
  • Recognize the symptoms of hypertension.
  • List factors that influence blood pressure.
  • Explain how to take blood pressure measurements.

    • Disclaimer

    This tutorial does not provide medical advice, diagnosis or treatment. This content is not intended to be a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay in seeking it because of something you have read on this tutorial.
